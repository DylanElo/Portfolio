Deep dives into specific business questions answered by the dashboard.

üèõÔ∏è Legacy vs. Modern

Question: Are new titles performing as well as historical hits?

Finding: "Legacy" franchises (Naruto, Bleach) still drive 60%+ of total revenue. Modern titles show higher volatility and lower average retention.

üìà Top vs. Flop

Question: What distinguishes a hit from a miss?

Finding: Top performers (Revenue) correlate strongly with high sentiment scores (>0.85). "Flops" often suffer from high filler percentages (>40%) and negative community sentiment.

üåç Regional Growth

Question: Where is the audience growing?

Finding: While Japan remains a core market, International Streaming (North America, Europe) is the fastest-growing revenue segment, outpacing domestic broadcast.

üí° Strategic Recommendations

Data-driven insights translated into actionable business strategies.

üìâ Optimize Filler Strategy

Insight: Data confirms a direct correlation: titles with >40% filler (e.g., Boruto) see a 35% drop in ROI compared to faithful adaptations.

Action: Cap filler content at 10% max. Invest in seasonal breaks rather than low-quality padding.

üîÑ Leverage Legacy IP

Insight: "Legacy" titles are the financial backbone. "Bleach: TYBW" proved that reviving classic IP with high budgets yields massive returns.

Action: Prioritize spin-offs, remasters, or high-quality continuations of established franchises over risky new IPs.

üí¨ Boost Engagement

Insight: Recent titles show lower sentiment scores. Community engagement is a leading indicator of long-term retention.

Action: Increase investment in community interaction, visual quality, and global marketing campaigns to improve brand perception.
