üö® The "Filler Trap"

Insight: Data confirms a direct correlation: titles with >40% filler (e.g., Boruto) see a 35% drop in ROI compared to faithful adaptations.

Action: Cap filler content at 10% max. Invest in seasonal breaks rather than low-quality padding.

üíé Quality Over Quantity

Insight: "Bleach: TYBW" (0% filler, high budget) achieved 3x the revenue per episode of long-running weekly formats.

Action: Pivot production model to high-budget, 12-24 episode seasonal cours.

üåç Global Streaming First

Insight: 65% of revenue now comes from international streaming (Crunchyroll/Netflix), yet marketing spend is still 80% domestic Japan.

Action: Reallocate marketing budget to target global digital channels immediately.

Architecture

The project follows a complete end-to-end BI pipeline:

  • Advanced Data Modeling: Implemented a robust Star Schema (Facts & Dimensions) in SQLite to handle complex analytical queries.
  • Large-Scale Simulation: Generated over 150,000+ rows of realistic daily performance data, including seasonality, marketing campaigns, and platform splits.
  • ETL Pipeline: Python-based ETL process to extract, transform, and load data, aggregating it into a high-performance JSON structure for the frontend.
  • Interactive Dashboard: A feature-rich dashboard built with Chart.js and Tailwind CSS, featuring multi-axis trend lines, heatmaps, and dynamic filtering.
