# Executive Requirements: Studio Pierrot BI Initiative

## 1. Executive Summary
**Objective:** Reverse the trend of declining mindshare for Studio Pierrot's legacy IPs compared to modern competitors (MAPPA, ufotable) by leveraging data-driven insights to optimize production quality, streaming distribution, and fan engagement.

**Key Stakeholders:**
- CEO / Board of Directors
- Head of Production
- Head of Global Distribution
- Chief Marketing Officer

## 2. Strategic Goals
1.  **Revitalize Legacy IPs:** Identify which "sleeping giants" (e.g., Bleach, Tokyo Ghoul) have sufficient pent-up demand to warrant high-budget revivals.
2.  **Optimize Production Models:** Determine the ROI of "Seasonal" (high quality, gaps) vs. "Continuous" (lower quality, steady revenue) production models.
3.  **Close the Streaming Gap:** Analyze competitor performance on Netflix/Crunchyroll to adjust licensing and release strategies.

## 3. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Global Demand Index:** Target >30x average demand for flagship titles (Benchmark: JJK 71.2x).
- **Production Quality Score:** Target >8.50 MAL score for new releases (Benchmark: Bleach TYBW 8.99).
- **Revenue Momentum:** Achieve >15% YoY growth in streaming revenue for back-catalog titles.

## 4. Dashboard Requirements
- **High-Level View:** Single-page executive summary with "Red/Green" status indicators for key IPs.
- **Trend Analysis:** 5-year revenue and popularity trend lines to identify inflection points.
- **Competitor Benchmarking:** Direct comparison against MAPPA and ufotable on key metrics (Score, Members, Revenue).

## 5. Success Criteria
- Delivery of a fully functional BI dashboard by Q4 2025.
- Identification of at least 2 actionable opportunities for IP revival or strategic pivot.
- Adoption of data-driven decision-making in greenlighting new productions.
