# Stakeholder Requirements: Cross-Functional BI Needs

## 1. Production Team
**User Persona:** Head of Production, Anime Producers
**Needs:**
- **Budget Optimization:** Correlate production budget/time with critical reception and revenue.
- **Filler Analysis:** Quantify the negative impact of "filler" episodes on viewer retention and MAL scores.
- **Studio Benchmarking:** Compare Pierrot's animation quality metrics against MAPPA and ufotable.

**Required Views:**
- "Production Quality vs. Score" Scatter Plot.
- "Filler % vs. Retention" Correlation Matrix.

## 2. Marketing & Distribution Team
**User Persona:** Global Marketing Manager, Licensing Director
**Needs:**
- **Regional Demand:** Identify which regions (NA, LATAM, EU) have the highest demand for specific IPs.
- **Platform Performance:** Track availability and popularity on Netflix vs. Crunchyroll vs. Hulu.
- **Fandom Sentiment:** Monitor real-time MyAnimeList score trends and member growth.

**Required Views:**
- "Global Fandom Map" (Geographic demand).
- "Streaming Availability" Matrix.
- "Sentiment Tracker" (Score evolution over time).

## 3. Finance & Strategy Team
**User Persona:** CFO, Strategic Planner
**Needs:**
- **Revenue Forecasting:** Project future revenue based on current IP momentum and market growth.
- **Portfolio Health:** Categorize IPs into "Cash Cows" (Legacy), "Stars" (New Hits), "Question Marks" (Potential Revivals), and "Dogs" (Declining).
- **ROI Analysis:** Compare revenue per episode across different production models.

**Required Views:**
- "Revenue Timeline (2020-2024)" Line Chart.
- "Portfolio Matrix" (BCG Matrix adaptation).
