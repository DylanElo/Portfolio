# Stakeholder Requirements Document
## Japan Inbound Travel Intelligence Hub

### 1. Role & Context
**Role**: BI Analyst at a major Japan DMC (e.g., EXO Japan, JTB, HIS).
**Mission**: Empower Sales, Product, and Operations teams with data-driven insights to optimize market targeting, product development, and resource planning.

### 2. Key Stakeholders

#### Head of Sales (Global)
*   **Goals**: Identify high-potential markets and segments.
*   **Key Questions**:
    *   Which markets (KR, CN, US, EU, ASEAN, AUS) should be prioritized this season?
    *   What is the breakdown of repeaters vs. first-timers?
    *   How are leisure vs. business segments performing?

#### Product Director
*   **Goals**: Develop relevant travel products and focus on trending regions.
*   **Key Questions**:
    *   Which regions are gaining popularity (Hokkaid≈ç, Kyushu, Tohoku, Kansai)?
    *   What is the trend in length of stay (short vs. long haul)?

#### Ops / Planning Manager
*   **Goals**: Manage inventory and staffing to prevent overload and ensure smooth operations.
*   **Key Questions**:
    *   When are the risks of overload (Golden Week, Obon, Cherry Blossom)?
    *   How do FX fluctuations impact demand from specific regions (e.g., weak Yen boosting US/EU)?
    *   Are there weather risks (Typhoons, Heatwaves) impacting specific dates?

### 3. Business Questions & Analytical Needs

#### Volume & Market Mix
*   How is the number of foreign visitors evolving by **Country**, **Region**, and **Month**?
*   Which markets have fully recovered post-COVID, and which are lagging?

#### Seasonality & Weather
*   What are the peak seasons for specific markets (e.g., Australians in Winter for ski, Europeans in Summer)?
*   How do extreme weather events (heatwaves, typhoons) correlate with visitor dips?

#### FX & Purchasing Power
*   Correlation between JPY exchange rates (vs USD, EUR, KRW, AUD, THB) and visitor volume.
*   Does a weaker Yen immediately drive volume from price-sensitive markets?

#### Capacity & Operations
*   **Flight Volume**: Daily/Weekly inbound flights to major gateways (NRT, HND, KIX, CTS, FUK).
*   **Stress Points**: Identification of "Red Zones" where High Flight Volume + Bad Weather + Peak Season converge.
